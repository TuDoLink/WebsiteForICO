What is TuDoLink?

A Sharing Platform

TuDoLink is a secure, transparent and decentralised platform​ where users can share, rent, lease and auction idle computing resources and their data. Researchers and developers save money when running experiments; Providers make money when sharing idle computing power or data.

A Future Investment Platform

Trading on TuDoLink, all parties will, directly or indirectly, contribute to the R&D. This is because our initial buyers will be predominantly scientists, researchers, students, medical doctors and so on.
One obvious example will be, if R&Ders need to process big data for a cancer research, instead of paying expensive cloud services or supercomputers, they can simply log onto ToDuLink to buy abundantly cheap or free computing power. Providers are helping to advance cancer treatment research. They may get a free cancer check or health tips from researchers.

A Super Networking Computer

Usres could build a distributed network computing system on TuDoLink, which could contain thousands of CPUs and GPUs. The system could be used for your projects, researches, experiments or programs and so on.

A Blockchain

Codes of tasks, data and transactions are all stored on the Blockchain. Suppliers can take tasks and trace codes of tasks whenever they want. Suppliers can also share and sell their data on the blockchain.

FAQ

What are differences from exist computing sharing projects?

- Easy: We use in-browser implementation technology. Users can share computing resources on ToDuLink by just 1 click of a button on the browser, no need for any additional hardware or software.
- Transparency: Codes of tasks traceable on blockchain.
- Social: ​Free communication between R&Ders and suppliers.
- Secure: ​Program Guarantee. ToDuLink also provides insurance for financial loss. That is how confident we are about our technology.

Do you have a strong tech team?

Yes. Our tech is supported by a strong team of university professors, post-doctorals and PhDs, embodying academic rigor and technological frontier. All their information can be found on our website.

Could you show me an example case?

One obvious example will be, if R&Ders need to process big data for a cancer research, instead of paying expensive cloud services or supercomputers, they can simply log onto ToDuLink to buy abundantly cheap or free computing power.
For the providers, they know exactly that they are helping to advance cancer treatment research. Moreover, they may get a free cancel check or health tips from the researchers.

What is your vision?

Our vision is to build a blockchain-based marketplace for people to trade computing resources and data around the world.
